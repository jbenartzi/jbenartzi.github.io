The Workshop

Recently, there have been substantial advancements in our understanding of the long-time dynamics of systems in kinetic theory, in particular the Vlasov-Poisson and Vlasov-Maxwell systems, as well as other related systems.

This workshop aims to bring together mathematicians, physicists and engineers interested in these problems to exchange ideas, seek ways forward, and foster new collaborations. A particular priority is to bring together both junior and senior colleagues.
