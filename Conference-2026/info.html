Location

The workshop will take place in the center of Rome, in a conference venue belonging to Tor Vergata Univerity. The address is

    Via Lucullo, 11, 00187 Roma

It is a 10-15 minute walk to Piazza della Repubblica and Termini Station to the southeast, 15-20 minute walk to the Historic Center to the southwest and a 10 minute walk to Villa Borghese to the north.
