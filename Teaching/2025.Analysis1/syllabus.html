Syllabus

We shall cover the following topics:

  1. Basic notions:
    • Set theory
    • Logic
    • Basic properties of functions
    • Vectors
    • Complex numbers
  2. Limits and continuity:
    • Limits of sequences
    • Limits of functions
    • Properties of limits
  3. Properties of functions:
    • Local properties
    • Global properties
  4. Derivatives
  5. Taylor expansions
  6. Integrals
