Syllabus

Our broad topics shall be:

  1. Introduction to Partial Differential Equations (PDEs)
  2. Two fundamental equations:
    The Wave Equation
    The Diffusion Equation (also known as the Heat Equation)
  3. Boundary Value Problems
  4. Fourier Series
  5. A third fundamental equation:
    Laplace's Equation

The above is the examinable material. Time permitting, we will cover additional topics of interest (which will not feature in the exam), such as

  • More equations coming from physics
  • Wave phenomena and variants of the wave equation
  • Nonlinear PDEs
