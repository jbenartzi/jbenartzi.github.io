Location

The conference will be held at the South Kensington Campus, located in central London. The Department of Mathematics is located at the Huxley Building, 180 Queen's Gate, London, SW7 2AZ.

The nearest tube lines are the Piccadilly and District lines. The Piccadilly line extends to Heathrow Airport in the west, and to King's Cross Train Station in the east (this is where Eurostar trains arrive from Paris and Brussels).

All talks will take place on the first floor of the building, at room 144. The mathematics department is located on the 6th floor, and the mathematics common room (this is where you can get coffee) is located on the 5th floor.

Accommodation

Imperial College provides an accommodation service which can help you find accommodation in the vicinity of the College for a reduced rate. You can contact the accommodation service through their website, by phone at +44 (0) 20 7594 9511, or by email at reservations@imperial.ac.uk. Be sure to mention that you are attending the conference.

A cheaper alternative is to book Imperial College dormitory rooms which can even be shared. More information is available here.

If you prefer to book a hotel independently, we recommend looking for hotels near Piccadilly or District line stations. For instance, the Hammersmith area is quite convenient.

If your expenses are being covered (fully or in part), contact us to arrange for a reimbursement. Please keep all your original receipts, as these will be required.
