Research interests:

  • Analysis, and in particular:
    • Spectral theory
    • Approximations of infinite-dimensional problems
  • PDEs, and in particular:
    • Kinetic theory
    • Long time behavior
  • Ergodic theory & Continuous time dynamical systems
